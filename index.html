About

Who We Are

AI-Orchestrated Operations

Nexagentis is an AI-driven digital business group headquartered in Sydney, Australia. Founded by Werner Kleynhans, we operate multiple digital ventures — each targeting a distinct vertical — unified by shared AI infrastructure and intelligent automation.

Our approach is simple: identify high-potential digital verticals, launch purpose-built brands, and scale operations through AI-orchestrated workflows. Every department benefits from centralised data pipelines, automated content production, and real-time decision intelligence.

We're not a startup chasing a single idea. We're a portfolio of focused digital businesses, each designed to operate lean and grow independently — with the shared advantage of group-wide AI capability.
