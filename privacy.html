Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Nexagentis ("we", "our", "us") — ABN 17 732 147 275, based in Sydney, Australia — collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you access our websites, applications, and services ("Services").

By using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Google User Data — Third-Party API Data Disclosure

This section specifically addresses how Nexagentis applications access, use, store, and share data obtained through Google API Services, in compliance with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

1. Google User Data Access

Nexagentis applications may request access to your Google account data through the following Google APIs:

  • YouTube Data API — to manage YouTube channel content, upload and manage videos, view channel analytics, and manage video metadata on your behalf.
  • Google Sheets API — to read from and write to Google Sheets used for business operations, including sales tracking, inventory management, and operational data.
  • Google Drive API — to access, create, and manage files in Google Drive for document storage, report generation, and business file management.
  • Gmail API — to send and manage emails for business communications, automated notifications, and operational messaging.

2. How We Use Google User Data

Data accessed through Google APIs is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Automated business workflow management — orchestrating operations across our digital ventures, including content publishing schedules, inventory updates, and business process automation.
  • Content publishing and management — uploading, scheduling, and managing video content on YouTube channels operated by Nexagentis ventures.
  • Sales and operations tracking — reading and writing operational data in Google Sheets for internal business management.
  • Business communications — sending operational emails, notifications, and reports through Gmail.
  • File management — storing and organising business documents and reports in Google Drive.

Nexagentis's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. How We Store Google User Data

  • Google user data is processed in real-time through automated workflows and is not stored beyond operational necessity.
  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are stored securely and are used solely to maintain authorised API access.
  • We do not store copies of your YouTube videos, Gmail messages, Google Drive files, or Google Sheets data beyond what is immediately necessary for processing.
  • Any cached or temporary data is automatically purged after the operational task is completed.

4. How We Share Google User Data

  • We do not sell, rent, or share Google user data with third parties.
  • Google user data is used only for the business owner's own account management — the data accessed is used to manage the authenticated user's own Google accounts and services.
  • We do not share Google user data with any advertising networks, data brokers, or other third-party services.
  • Google user data may only be disclosed if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

5. Revoking Google Data Access

You can revoke Nexagentis's access to your Google account data at any time by:

  • Visiting your Google Account permissions page and removing Nexagentis from the list of connected apps.
  • Contacting us at privacy@nexagentis.com.au to request data deletion.

Upon revocation, we will promptly delete all stored tokens and cease accessing your Google account data.

6. General Information Collection

Beyond Google API data, Nexagentis may collect information in the following ways:

6.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact information (name, email address) when you communicate with us.
  • Business information you voluntarily submit through our platforms.
  • Payment information processed through secure third-party payment processors.

6.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser type, device information, and operating system.
  • IP address and approximate geographic location.
  • Pages visited, time spent, and navigation patterns on our websites.
  • Referral source and search terms used to find our sites.

6.3 Cookies and Tracking

Our websites may use cookies and similar technologies for analytics, site functionality, and user experience improvement. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. How We Use General Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve our Services.
  • To communicate with you regarding your inquiries or transactions.
  • To analyse website usage and improve user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • To protect the security and integrity of our Services.

8. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers — trusted third parties that assist in operating our business (hosting, analytics, payment processing), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements — when disclosure is required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice provided to affected users.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

10. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL).
  • Secure credential storage and access controls.
  • Regular security reviews and updates.
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@nexagentis.com.au.

12. Australian Privacy Act 1988

Nexagentis is committed to compliance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). As an Australian business, we handle personal information in accordance with these requirements. If you believe we have breached the APPs, you may lodge a complaint by contacting us directly, or by contacting the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

13. Children's Privacy
